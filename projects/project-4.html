Project Summary

The Digital Insurance Claims Platform streamlines the insurance claim process by enabling customers to submit claims digitally, upload supporting documents, complete identity verification, and monitor claim progress directly from their mobile devices. By combining artificial intelligence, image processing, and secure mobile technologies, the application improves claim accuracy while reducing manual processing and operational overhead.
