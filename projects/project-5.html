Workout Rewards App - iOS (Swift) & Android (Jetpack Compose)

Category : Health & Fitness
Client : Confidential
Role : Senior Mobile App Developer (iOS & Android)
Technology : Swift, Jetpack Compose, HealthKit, Health Connect, Firebase, WebSocket
Feature : Health Data Integration, Offline-first Sync, Gamified Rewards, Real-time Updates, Firebase Notifications

  • The Workout Rewards App motivates users by tracking workouts and rewarding achievements, ensuring a seamless experience across both iOS and Android.
  • On iOS, integrated Apple HealthKit for activity and health data; on Android, integrated Google Health Connect for fitness tracking.
  • Designed an offline-first architecture with local storage, background sync, and Firebase for authentication, cloud sync, and push notifications.
  • Implemented real-time progress updates using WebSocket, keeping data consistent across devices instantly.
  • Developed modern and engaging UI/UX with SwiftUI for iOS and Jetpack Compose for Android.
  • As a Senior Mobile App Developer, I was responsible for end-to-end development on both platforms, including architecture design, core feature implementation, code quality, and performance optimization.
