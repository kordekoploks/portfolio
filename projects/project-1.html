Project Summary

BTPN Syariah Mobile Banking is an enterprise banking application designed to support field officers in delivering secure financial services to customers across Indonesia. The application enables end-to-end banking operations including loan disbursement, loan repayment, savings management, customer verification, offline transactions, and secure synchronization with enterprise banking systems. It is optimized for field operations where internet connectivity may be limited, ensuring uninterrupted financial services in remote areas.
