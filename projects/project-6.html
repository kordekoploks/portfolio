Project Summary

UrZiggurat is a modern mobile platform that enables customers to securely buy, sell, and manage precious metals through a seamless digital experience. Built for users across the United Arab Emirates and Iraq, the application delivers secure financial services with complete English and Arabic localization, including native Right-to-Left (RTL) support.
